This bill would require that the political party with more members in the House have a majority (more than half) of the seats on certain standing committees.

The biggest impact will be felt by members of Congress and their staff. It could also affect which bills are prioritized and how they’re shaped within these committees. The bill doesn't specify *which* standing committees would be affected, so that detail is important to understand when looking at the full text.

Supporters argue that this change would make House committees more representative of the overall composition of the House and could prevent minority viewpoints from being completely ignored.
