WHO IS AFFECTED:
- Members of the House of Representatives.
- Committee chairs and party leadership in the House. 

WHAT CHANGES:
- The Steering Committee would have less direct control over appointments.
- More members could directly vote on who serves on these committees.
- The Ways & Means and Rules Committees will have a larger, more diverse membership.

WHAT'S DEBATED:
